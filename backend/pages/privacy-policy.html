Privacy Policy

Your privacy and security are our top priorities

Last updated: October 2024

🛡️ Data Protection

We are committed to protecting your personal information and privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Information We Collect

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information
  • Usage Data: Connection logs, chat duration, feature usage
  • Media Access: Camera and microphone (only during active calls)
  • No Personal Data: We don't collect names, emails, or personal details

🎥 Video & Audio Privacy

🚫 No Recording

We never record, store, or save your video or audio conversations

🔐 End-to-End

All video calls are peer-to-peer with no server intermediary

⏱️ Real-time Only

Data is transmitted in real-time and not stored anywhere

🎭 Anonymous

No user accounts or personal identification required

🔐 Security Measures

Advanced AI-powered content filtering to detect and block inappropriate content automatically

Real-time monitoring for suspicious or harmful behavior patterns
